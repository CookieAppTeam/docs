## ‚ùì General Questions

??? quote "What does Cookie do?"
    Cookie offers various features for your server, such as games, tickets, giveaways, leveling,
    temporary voice channels, and stats. 

    Use `/help` to explore all features.

??? quote "Is Cookie free to use?"
    Yes, all features are available for free. With Cookie Premium, you can increase limits.

??? quote "Can I customize Cookie's messages?"
    Yes, use the `/messages` command to personalize messages sent by Cookie.

??? quote "Why isn't a command showing up?"
    Some commands are only available on servers, not in private chats.
    If you are on a server, ensure that Cookie is a member of the server, and 
    you have the required permissions, as some commands can only be used by server admins.

??? quote "Where can I report bugs or suggest features?"
    You can report bugs easily using `/bug`. 
    You can also join our [Support Server](https://cookieapp.me/support) and create a new post 
    in our `#‚ùìcookie-support` forum.


## üç™ Economy

??? quote "How do I earn cookies?"
    You can earn cookies by using claiming your `/daily` reward. 
    You can get a job with `/work` or use your oven with `/bake`.
    
??? quote "What can I do with cookies?"
    You can spend cookies on various items in the `/shop`.

??? quote "Is the economy system global or per-server?"
    The economy system is global. Your cookies and items are shared across all servers 
    where you use Cookie.


## üíé Cookie Premium

??? quote "How do I subscribe to Cookie Premium?"
    Inside Discord, open [Cookie's profile](https://discord.com/application-directory/1005119817294041209/store) 
    and click on the `Shop` button to view available subscriptions.

??? quote "What are the benefits of Cookie Premium?"
    While all features of Cookie are free to use, Cookie Premium offers increased limits on 
    certain features, such as more ticket categories or giveaway restrictions.

??? quote "Can I test premium features before subscribing?"
    Yes, you can try Cookie Premium free for 30 days using the `/premium info` command.

??? quote "Is the subscription user-based or server-based?"
    Once you subscribe, you can use premium features on all servers. If you own a server,
    your members will also be able to use premium features on your server.
