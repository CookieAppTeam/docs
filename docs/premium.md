# Cookie Premium

You can use **all features** of Cookie completely for free. 
However, you can support Cookie's development to increase some limits and help keep the bot running!

## Premium Perks
By subscribing to Cookie Premium, you will get access to the following perks:

- Increased Limits (more ticket categories, more giveaway restrictions, ...)
- A special role on our [Support Server](https://cookieapp.me/support)

## How does Cookie Premium work?
It's simple!

- Once you buy Cookie Premium, you can use premium features on **all servers**!
- If you own a server, your **members** will be able to use premium features on your server!

!!! info "Premium Status"
    To view your current premium status, use the command `/premium info`.

## Subscribe to Cookie Premium
Simply open [Cookie's profile](https://discord.com/application-directory/1005119817294041209/store) within Discord. 
Click on the `Shop` button to view available subscriptions.

## Free Trial
You can try Cookie Premium free for **30 days** by using the command `/premium info`.

You don't need to provide any payment information and the trial will automatically expire after 30 days.
